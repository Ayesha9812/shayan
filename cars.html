A car is a vehicle that people use to travel from one place to another on roads.

Simple words:

  • A car has 4 wheels
  • It runs on petrol, diesel, electricity, or gas
  • It carries people and small luggage
  • It is driven on roads

Common types of cars:

  • Sedan – normal family car
  • SUV – bigger, strong, good for rough roads
  • Hatchback – small city car
  • Sports car – very fast, stylish
