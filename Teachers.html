Students' Needs

Students have different kinds of needs that must be met for effective learning:

  1. Academic needs
  • Clear explanations
  • Appropriate learning materials
  • Feedback to improve performance
  • Support for different learning styles and abilities
  1. Emotional needs
  • Feeling safe and respected
  • Encouragement and motivation
  • Encouragement and motivation
  • Confidence-building and understanding
  1. Social needs
  • Opportunities to interact and collaborate
  • Fair treatment and inclusion
  • Sense of belonging in the classroom
  1. Physical needs
  • Comfortable learning environment
  • Adequate breaks, lighting, and seating
