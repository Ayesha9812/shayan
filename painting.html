  • Painting is the art of making pictures using colorson a surface like paper, canvas, or a wall
  • It’s a way to show ideas, feelings, nature, people, or imagination through colors and shapes.

    • How painting is done (simple steps)

    1. Choose a surface – paper, canvas, wall
    2. Choose colors – watercolor, acrylic, oil, poster colors
    3. Use tools – brush, sponge, fingers, knife
    4. Paint – make lines, shapes, and fill colors
    5. Let it dry – some paints dry fast, some slow

    Types of painting

    • Watercolor – soft, light colors (used with water)
    • Acrylic – bright, dries fast
    • Oil painting – very smooth, dries slowly
    • Wall painting / mural – painting on walls

    Why people like painting

    • It’s creative
    • It makes you relaxed
    • You can express feelings
    • Anyone can learn it
